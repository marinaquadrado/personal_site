Fiz um ano de Design de Comunicação em Faro, na Universidade do Algarve (2011-2012), mas acabei por me mudar para Lisboa e licenciei-me em Design de Comunicação na Faculdade de Belas Artes da Universidade de Lisboa (2012-2015).

My name is Marina Quadrado and I am a twenty three years old designer.

I started my formation in the field in Escola Secundária Gabriel Pereira (Évora) where I developed my interest for design (2008-2011).

I made a year of Communication Design in Faro, in the Algarve University (2011-2012), but I ended up moving for Lisbon and did my course in Communication Design in the Faculty of Fine Arts of the University of Lisboa (2012-2015).

I do a little bit of everything but my interest is in areas like Editorial design, Webdesign, Graphic design, Typography, Illustration, Photography and Video.
